Travel AutoLog - Privacy Policy

Privacy-First Approach: Travel AutoLog is designed with privacy as a core principle. All your work data stays on your device. Optional telemetry is anonymous and helps us improve the app.

What Information We Collect

Local Data (Stays on Your Device)

  • Work Entries: Job details, time tracking, reports you create
  • Settings: Your preferences, export directories, language settings
  • GPS Data: Location tracking for work routes (if enabled)
  • User Profile: Name, work address, email preferences

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.

Optional Anonymous Telemetry

With your consent, we may collect:

  • Crash Reports: Anonymous error logs to identify and fix bugs
  • Performance Metrics: App performance data to optimize user experience
  • Feature Usage: Anonymous statistics about which features are used
  • Device Information: Operating system version, device type (no personal identifiers)

Your Privacy Rights

Full Control

  • Opt-Out Anytime: Disable telemetry in app settings
  • Export Your Data: Download all your local app data as JSON
  • Delete Everything: Remove all app data from your device
  • No Account Required: The app works completely offline

Data Security

  • Local Storage: Your work data is stored locally using encrypted browser storage
  • Anonymous Telemetry: No personal identifiers are included in crash reports
  • No User Accounts: No servers store your personal information
  • HTTPS Only: All network communication uses secure encryption

Third-Party Services

Optional Monitoring (Sentry)

When telemetry is enabled, we use Sentry for crash reporting:

  • All personally identifiable information is scrubbed
  • Only anonymous error logs and performance metrics are sent
  • View Sentry's privacy policy: sentry.io/privacy

Maps (Optional)

If you use GPS features, map tiles may be loaded from Mapbox:

  • Only required for displaying maps in the app
  • Your GPS coordinates are processed locally
  • View Mapbox privacy policy: mapbox.com/legal/privacy

Children's Privacy

Travel AutoLog is designed for professional use by adults. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. Users will be notified of significant changes through the app.

Contact Information
